Policies of Service:

Client Policies:
  1. Collateral will be a minimal of 10% of asset market value (Jita).
  2. Collateral will be no more than 100% of asset market value (Jita).
  3. No assembled containers allowed.

Pilot Policies:

  1. Service does not replace assets due to lose or theft.
  2. All high-sec and low-sec transport is to be done using out of corp/alliance/bloc alts.
  3. Are responsible for confirming all properties of a contract before accepting contact.
  4. Before being allowed to accept contacts pilots must complete the `safty first` training.

Recommendations:

  1. It is HIGHLY recommended to have a high/low sec alt to complete contacts outside of null.
  2. Service recommends Deep Space Transport in lue of Freighter in high sec.
  3. `High sec` alts should stay in the NPC corp to avoid war-dec situations.
